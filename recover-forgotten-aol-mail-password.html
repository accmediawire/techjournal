How to Recover a forgotten AOL mail password

Looking to recover or reset your AOL mail password?

In this article, you learn how to reset your forgotten email password and all these instructions apply to the desktop version of AOL mail

AOL mail password reset instructions

Steps to reset the AOL password using a web browser on your computer:

  1. Open up AOL sign-in page on your computer browser.
  2. Put your AOL username.
  3. Click Next.
  4. Now click I forgot my password.
  5. Choose the password reset recovery options linked to your AOL mail account.

    Note:- if you do not have the access to your recovery email or phone number click 'I need more options'.
  6. Enter the code sent to your recovery phone number or email address.
  7. Now it will allow you to create a new password for AOL mail.

    Below you can see the tips to help remember your passwords.
  8. Enter the verification code you received on your recovery phone or the email address and click Verify.
  9. Log into your AOL mail account using the new password you just created.

If you are unable to remember the password for your AOL account you can simply go ahead and check your browser information using the autofill feature of the browser. The option will arise after entering the username on the AOL site and if you have previously use the AOL mail on the browser and did save your password, then it will give you a pop up with your password and this will be the best way to recover your password and later you can reset it. 

If not, go through this article and it will help you to understand how you can reset your password using the different recovery options or without one.

Using the recovery information

AOL has recently switched from password recovery and will be providing you just two methods to reset your password that is your recovery phone number or the email address that you have added while setting up your AOL account.

How to reset an AOL mail password without recovery email ID 

Follow this simple guide step by step to reset your AOL mail password without recovery email ID (using the recovery phone number).

  1. Open a web browser such as Google Chrome or Firefox and go to the AOL mail login page.
  2. Put your username and click Continue.
  3. The next page asks for your password, here you have to click on the I forgot my password option.
  4. On the next page it will come up with some digits of the phone number linked to your account, there you have to click on Yes, text me a verification code.
  5. Check your cell phone for the code received from AOL and enter it on your computer and click Verify.
  6. If everything goes right it will redirect you to a page that will allow you to set a new password for your AOL account

    Now enter the new password and make sure it is strong enough and click Continue.
  7. Go back to the able mail sign-in page and use the new password you just created to sign in to your AOL mail account.

How to reset an AOL mail password without the recovery phone number

If you do not have the access to your recovery phone number which is linked to your AOL mail account, you can reset it using the recovery email address.

We have added the best steps to help you reset your AOL mail password without the recovery phone number. Follow this simple guide step by step to reset your AOL mail password without recovery phone number (using the recovery email ID).

  1. Go to the AOL mail login page on a browser.
  2. Enter the username and click Next.
  3. Now click I forgot my password underneath the sign-in button.
  4. Then it will come up with the recovery email ID that you have added as a recovery option in your AOL mail. Select the recovery email and click Next to get a verification code on your recovery email from AOL.
  5. Copy the verification code from your email and paste it on your AOL website where it was asking for the code and click the Continue button.
  6. The next page will allow you to set a new password make sure you are adding a strong password and after putting it twice, click Continue.
  7. In the next option it will come up with the recovery options that you can update like a phone number or email address or simply click on Remind me later.

If all the steps are done correctly, your password should be changed to a newer one. Log out from AOL mail and try to log in again using the new password you just created.

All these steps can help you regain access to your AOL mail if you want to reset your AOL mail password.

Tips to create secure passwords for your AOL mail

We have added the best tips that you can use to create stronger passwords for your online accounts.

  1. Do not include your phone number, or name in your password
  2. Always make sure to add special characters in your passwords(in the beginning or at the end).
  3. Leave out spaces between words in your passwords.
  4. Do not save your password in your computer browser.
  5. Avoid using the sequence numbers or anything obvious.
  6. The longer the password the most secure and safe it is from hackers.
  7. Avoid using the same passwords on all the sites, make a unique password for each site.
  8. Do not create your passwords based on your details such as the birth date, phone number, and address.
  9. Make sure to change your password every 40-60 days.

How to recover an AOL mail account without the phone number or email address

Are you trying to get access to your AOL mail account without the recovery options?

If you do not have the access to the recovery options such as your phone number or email address you may not be able to regain access to your AOL account. With the help of your recovery methods, AOL Verifies that you are the rightful owner of the account and it belongs to you by entering the verification code which is generally sent to the recovery phone number or email address. In this case, you may go ahead and contact the AOL experts on call to get across the verification process and to get back access to your mail account.
