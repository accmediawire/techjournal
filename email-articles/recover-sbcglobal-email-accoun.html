SBCGlobal email is one of the most prominent and significant email service providers in the United States. SBC Global communication has recently partnered with AT&T which makes it much more effective.

HOW TO RESET FORGOTTEN SBC GLOBAL EMAIL ACCOUNT PASSWORD?

Forgetting an email password or not being able to sign in to your email account is a very common issue faced by many users. This article will teach you to step-by-step process how you can recover a forgotten sbcglobal email account password.

METHOD- I: BY GENERATING SECURITY QUESTIONS

  1. Click this link https://m.att.com/my/#/forgotLoginLanding and it will take you to the password recovery page.
  2. Click the checkbox that says 'Password' and then enter your username and email address. After that, provide your 'name' there and click on 'Continue' button.
  3. Select ‘I’ll answer my security question’ in the next step for recovery options.
  4. Now select your choice of secret questions and set your answers, and then click 'Continue'.
  5. SBC Global will start to verify your data and if the data matches correctly, then you will get the option to create a new passsword for your email account.
  6. Make sure to create strong password and then click on the ' Continue' button to complete the process.

METHOD- II: BY GETTING A TEMPORARY PASSWORD

  1. Follow above-mentioned first two steps.
  2. Click this link https://m.att.com/my/#/forgotLoginLanding and it will take you to the password recovery page.
  3. Click the checkbox that says 'Password' and then enter your username and email address. After that, provide your 'name' there and click on 'Continue' button.
  4. Select ‘Send me a temporary password’ in the next step for recovery option.
  5. Enter an alternate email address there to get a temporary password for you sbcglobal email. And then click 'Continue' button.
  6. Get the temporary password from your alternate email inbox and put that password on the recovery page of SBC Global login.
  7. Now you should be logged in to the account and you can change your password using the account settings option. And setting up a strong password is essential as the temporary password is going to be valid for a short period only.

HOW TO RECOVER SBC GLOBAL EMAIL ACCOUNT PASSWORD?

  1. You need to head straight to the AT&T forgot password page.
  2. After that, choose the 'forgot password' option and enter the AT&T details it asks for.
  3. Enter the email address and last name.
  4. Click on the 'Continue' button and then choose ‘I’ll choose my security question’ if you remember your security question or click on 'Send me a temporary password from the drop-down menu.
  5. Answer all the security questions and click the 'Continue' button.
  6. Create a new password for SBC Global email account and reconfirm the password.
  7. Once you confirm the new password, it will take a minute and your new password will be ready to sign you into sbcglobal email.
